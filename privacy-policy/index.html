Count Keeper App Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 12 March 2025

Thank you for using our app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data—or rather, how we don't.

1. No Data Collection

Our app does not collect, store, or process any personal information. This means:

We don't gather names, email addresses, or contact details.

We don't access your location or device identifiers.

We don't track usage statistics or analytics.

Everything you do in the app stays on your device. The app works offline and doesn't send any information to us or anyone else.

2. No Cookies or Tracking

We don't use cookies, tracking tools, or any methods to monitor your behavior. Your use of the app is completely private.

3. No Third-Party Involvement

The app doesn't connect to third-party services that might collect data. There's no sharing or external processing—everything remains local to your device.

4. Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data beyond what's needed for the app to function locally (like settings or inputs you choose to save), there's no additional security risk. Your device's own protections (like passwords or locks) keep your local data safe.

5. Suitable for All Ages

The app is safe for everyone, including children, because no personal information is collected or shared.
